Summary
We are building a multi-tenant SaaS platform that optimizes packaging decisions for shipping operations. The system calculates the most efficient box configuration for items based on dimensions, fragility constraints, and available inventory — including multi-box joining logic.

Our target customers include 3PLs, specialty shippers, and eCommerce operators seeking to reduce dimensional weight costs and packaging waste.

This is an early-stage build with real commercial potential.
We are seeking a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to architect and build the core SaaS platform from the ground up.

This is not a simple web app.
The system includes:

Multi-tenant architecture

Optimization algorithms (3D fitting logic)

API-first design

Usage-based billing

Admin dashboards

Performance-sensitive logic

You will serve as the primary technical lead during MVP development.
